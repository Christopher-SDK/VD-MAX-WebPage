The main questions
our clients ask us

How to use?

First, you pour a little of the product already mixed with water, depending on the amount you are using. Then you wait 10 to 15 seconds, and that's it, you pass your sponge and all the dirt will come out automatically.

How much water has to be mixed with the product?

In case you are using products that contain 250 ml, it can be mixed with even 1 and a half liters of water, with industrial quality!

Can it be used neat?

Of course, however, we recommend our customers use them with water. This is because we do not want your products to end quickly, since what we provide is quantity and quality.

How do I choose a product?

The products we provide are effective and of quality. However, we recommend first knowing the area where we are going to clean.

Do the products damage the area where they are used?

No, since the products we offer are only designed to remove blemishes found in the areas where it is used.

Does it hurt your hands?

Absolutely not, our products are manufactured and made for free contact with the skin; but we always recommend to our public the use of gloves.
